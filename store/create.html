    This page is for creating products to list on the WFD Store.

    You will need an account on WhiskeyFlatDays.com to proceed. You may login or register. Once signed in, the Add Item button will be enabled.

    You may click on the {% include common/icon.html icon="info" height="18" width="18" fill="currentColor" %} icons for the fields to get more information.

    This is a temporary solution until sellers have accounts associated with their profiles. Please select one, or Contact KernValley.US {% include common/icon.html icon="link-external" height="18" width="18" fill="currentColor" %} to be added.
    Add a Product
    A short name for the product. 8 — 26 characters.
    A description for the product, consisting of 10 — 80 character
    Optional SKU, if available.

    Please select a 4:3 image of the product as a JPEG or PNG.


    Please select one or more categories. On Desktop, select multiple options by holding ctrl
    This is the amount you will receive on any sale of the item. Note: Final listing price will vary.
    Select one of In Stock, Back Order, etc.
    At this time, shipping must be a pre-determined amount. You may choose to include shipping cost in the price field, or you may list it as a separate charge here. You will recieve 100% of the shipping cost on every purchase.
